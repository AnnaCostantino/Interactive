Emma the Squirrel

A TikTok Phenomenon

Emma the squirrel is one of the beloved little wild residents featured on the @squirrel.boxes TikTok and social channels. She’s often seen relaxing in her cozy squirrel nesting box, enjoying her safe backyard home and showing off her curious, confident personality. Fans adore the calm scenes of Emma resting, watching her surroundings, and interacting with her environment — even giving friendly warnings when other squirrels get too close. Her quiet moments in the box capture a sense of trust and comfort, and her charm has made her a favorite among followers of the Squirrel Boxes videos.
