Sinan the Squirrel

A Local Squirrelebrity

I had the pleasure of meeting this little critter on his 6th birthday! How cute!

Sinan is a friendly, well-known squirrel from Tennessee who’s become an internet sensation thanks to his Instagram account @sinantn18. He was rescued as a baby and now lives with his owner, appearing in adorable photos and videos — often sporting tiny outfits and charming poses. Fans in Knoxville and beyond love seeing his adventures, from chilling on shoulders to roaming around with his personality on full display. Sinan’s cute, playful presence and social media charm have made him a beloved little celebrity among animal lovers online.
